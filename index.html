SPARK LEARNING

Project Spark is an innovative educational program that empowers children with special needs to unlock their full potential. Through a range of engaging and interactive activities, children are encouraged to study which they often find boring in special schools.

Learn More

The Problem

  • Limited educational resources tailored to the needs of children with Down syndrome Social isolation and lack of opportunities to connect with peers.
  • Low self-esteem and self-confidence due to perceived limitations and societal stigma.
  • Difficulty in accessing individualized support and guidance in academic and personal development.
  • Limited exposure to activities that foster creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills.
  • Lack of access to technology and adaptive devices that can facilitate.
